🔌 History of Electronics

Discover the fascinating evolution of electronics — a journey that begins with glowing vacuum tubes and stretches all the way to the ultra-compact chips powering the devices in your pocket today. From the first electric spark that lit up a bulb to the microprocessors that now drive smartphones, electronics has reshaped every corner of modern life. Explore groundbreaking inventions, meet the brilliant minds behind them, and see how each innovation paved the way for the next. Learn how simple circuits turned into complex computers, and how ideas born in dusty labs now live inside watches, homes, and even our bodies. Ready to explore, be inspired, and test your tech knowledge? Step into the timeline of innovation — and experience the story of electronics like never before.
